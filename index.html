Our Story

Thistle Lanka was created from a simple but powerful belief: skincare should be gentle, effective, and thoughtfully made.

What began as a personal passion slowly grew into a deeper commitment to understanding ingredients, skin science, and formulation. With an academic background in science and further studies in cosmetic technology, I approach skincare not only with creativity, but with knowledge and care.

Every product is handcrafted in small batches, combining botanical inspiration with a science-informed formulation process. From our Daily Care collection designed to address specific skin concerns, to our slow-crafted premium cold process soaps cured with patience, each bar reflects both heart and understanding.
