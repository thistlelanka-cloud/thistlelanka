3. Shipping Policy

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–3 working days after payment confirmation.

For bank transfers or deposits, processing begins only after proof of payment is verified by our team.

Delivery Time

Estimated delivery within Sri Lanka: 2–5 working days.

Please note: Delivery time may vary depending on your specific location and courier operations (especially during holidays or extreme weather).

Cash on Delivery (COD) Orders

  • Customers must be available at the provided contact number during the delivery window.
  • Failed delivery attempts due to unavailability or unreachable contact numbers may result in automatic order cancellation.

Incorrect Address

Customers are responsible for providing accurate delivery details. Additional charges will apply for re-delivery if the initial attempt fails due to incorrect information provided at checkout.
